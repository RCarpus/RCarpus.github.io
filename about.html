About Me

I completed my undergraduate and graduate studies in civil engineering at the university of Michigan in Ann Arbor, obtaining Bachelor of Science and Master of Science in Engineering degrees and completing my program in December of 2017. I have four years of engineering experience as a staff geotechnical engineer here in Ann Arbor, providing geotechnical services for new commercial developments, pavements, and utilities throughout southeast Michigan. I have also performed detailed design analyses for the design of foundation elements for photovoltaic solar power plants across the country.

Working in consulting, I’ve kept a client-centric mindset—that is, the client’s need is my need, and the client’s success is my success. I always strive to deliver the best of myself to my clients. That means producing high quality laboratory data, providing sound engineering judgement focusing on safety as well as the bottom line, and presenting a professional and confident attitude that keeps clients thinking of us for their future needs.

Through my time as a geotechnical engineer, I cultivated a passion for programming and technology that has culminated in my enrollment in a career-change program with CareerFoundry in an effort to transition into a web development role. I look forward to applying my engineering mindset to new problems and technologies to bring more value to your team!
