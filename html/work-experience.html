Staff Engineer: G2 Consulting Group

May 2017 to Present

After working a summer internship as a quality control field technician in 2017, I transitioned to a full-time role in January 2018 upon completion of both my Bachelor and Master of Science in Engineering degrees at the University of Michigan. I have served a variety of progressive roles here from providing field inspection services, laboratory testing, and detailed geotechnical analyses for projects ranging in scale from investigating the cause of excessive settlement in a local residential home to designing foundation elements for large scale solar power plants across the country. I also maintain our laboratory’s external quality control accreditation through AASHTO re:source, ensuring laboratory test results are performed consistently and correctly every time, regardless of which technician performed the work.

Working in consulting, I cultivated a client-centric mindset—that is, the client’s need is my need, and the client’s success is my success. I always strive to deliver the best of myself to my clients. That means producing high quality laboratory data, providing sound engineering judgement focusing on safety as well as the bottom line, and presenting a professional and confident attitude that keeps clients thinking of us for their future needs.
